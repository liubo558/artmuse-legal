ArtMuse Privacy Policy

AI Photo Editor - What You Say Is What You Get

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

ArtMuse ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the ArtMuse mobile application (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Apple ID Information: When you sign in with Apple, we receive only the information you choose to share
  • Voice Commands: Audio recordings are processed locally on your device and immediately deleted after processing
  • Photos: Photo files you choose to edit remain on your device and are never uploaded to our servers

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system version, and app version for compatibility purposes
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance (no personal data included)
  • Subscription Status: Information about your subscription status for Pro features

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, store, or access your edited photos
  • We do not retain voice recordings after processing
  • We do not collect location data
  • We do not track your browsing activity outside the app

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's functionality
  • Process your subscription payments through Apple
  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Provide customer support when requested
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With Apple: For App Store and payment processing
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights
  • Service Providers: With trusted partners who help operate our app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your photos and edits are stored locally on your device
  • Cloud Sync (Pro users only): Optional cloud backup of project files (not the actual media)
  • Security: We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data
  • Data Retention: Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to provide our services

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access and update your account information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Opt out of analytics data collection in device settings
  • Cancel your subscription at any time through Apple

7. Children's Privacy

ArtMuse is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will delete it immediately.

8. Third-Party Services

The App integrates with:

  • Apple Services: Sign in with Apple, In-App Purchases, CloudKit
  • AI Processing Services: For photo editing (data processed anonymously)

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any material changes through the App or by email. Your continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: privacy@artmuse.app

Support: Through the App's settings menu
