Frequently Asked Questions

How do I use voice commands?
Tap the microphone icon and speak your editing request. For example: "Make the sky more blue", "Remove that person", or "Change background to sunset".
Why am I getting "Monthly limit reached"?
Free users get 3 AI edits per month. Upgrade to Pro for 500 monthly edits, or wait until next month for your quota to reset.

All edited photos are automatically saved to your device's photo library. You can find them in the Photos app.
Why is AI processing taking so long?
AI processing typically takes 10-30 seconds. Complex edits may take longer. Pro users get priority processing for faster results.

Yes! Go to the subscription screen in the app and tap "Restore Purchases" to sync your Pro subscription across devices.

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

  • Voice not recognized: Speak clearly and ensure microphone permission is enabled
  • App crashes: Restart the app and ensure you have the latest version
  • Poor edit results: Try more specific commands or use higher quality source photos
  • Network errors: Check your internet connection as AI processing requires online access

Best Practices

  • Use high-quality photos for better AI results
  • Be specific with your voice commands
  • Ensure good lighting when taking new photos
  • Allow microphone and photo library permissions
